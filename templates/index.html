Cari tahu tentang haechan

Lee Haechan, atau dikenal juga dengan nama panggung Haechan, adalah salah satu idol Kpop yang populer. Ia merupakan anggota boy group NCT, dan sub-unit NCT Dream, NCT 127, dan NCT U.

On July 17, 2014, he was introduced as a member of the pre-debut trainee team SMROOKIES. In August 2014, he appeared on Exo 90:2014, a reality TV Show starring EXO, alongside other members of the SMROOKIES program, where they performed songs from the 1990s.
